Los Angeles (US: /lɔːs ˈændʒələs/ (About this sound listen);[18] Spanish for "The Angels"; Spanish: [los ˈaŋxeles]; officially: the City of Los Angeles; colloquially: by its initials L.A.) is the second-most populous city in the United States, after New York City. With an estimated population of almost four million,[19] Los Angeles is the largest and most populous city in the state of California and the cultural, financial, and commercial center of Southern California.
